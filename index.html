3 Guiding Principles
① Efficiency over ROAS
Sort decisions by campaign_sales / spend ratio, not ROAS% alone. A €75 spend with 12,278% ROAS is great but negligible in impact.
② Manage Drone at SKU level
Category average (40.9x) masks a 10x spread — Mini 5 Pro Fly More returns 86.8x while DJI Flip returns just 7.7x. Never allocate Drone as a block.
③ Mic & Action are underfunded
Both show CTR ≥ 0.64%, CVR ≥ 10%, and 45–68x efficiency. They are systematically starved of budget relative to demonstrated performance.
